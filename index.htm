Educational Background

I am an Mphil Management Information Systems Student in Information Systems at the University for Development of Studies. My research interests are broadly in ICT4D and specifically targeting digital technologies in business and innovations in society. My research interest has over the years focused on how to understand the nexuses in the ICT4D value chain (ICT readiness, access, use, impact) in different aspects of business and society using both qualitative and quantitative methodologies.
